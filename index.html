Arctic Fox

Arctic fox fur changes seasonly. fluffy white coats in winter, brownish or grayish in summer.

Scientific name: Vulpes Lagopus

Length: Up to 110 centimeters

Lifespan: 3-6 years

Habitat: Arctic and alphine tundra

A small fox that adapts well to living in cold environments, it is best known for it's thick and warm fur that is also used as camouflage.
